Our Story

How It Works

Built differently, on purpose

Every design decision we made was with one question in mind: is this good for the child? The answer shapes everything.

🤲

Handpicked Content

Every single video on our platform is individually reviewed by our team. No algorithm decides what your child sees — real humans do, every time.

🔒

Age-Gated by Design

Content is tagged and locked by age group. A 4-year-old simply cannot access content meant for a 10-year-old. It's enforced at the system level.

⚡

Short-Form Magic

Designed for short attention spans in the best way — engaging, educational micro-content that leaves them inspired, not endlessly scrolling.

🌍

India-First Creators

We champion Indian creators, educators, artists, and performers. Content that reflects Indian culture, languages, and the diverse world your child lives in.

📊

Parent Dashboard

Full visibility for parents. See what your child watched, how long, and set daily time limits — complete control without hovering over their shoulder.

🎓

Learning Built In

Entertainment that teaches. Every piece of content goes through an educational value check — fun is mandatory, but so is growth.

Safety First, Always

Zero compromise.
Zero tolerance.

Other platforms moderate at scale with algorithms. We moderate with people, process, and a parent's instinct. There is no grey area.

🧑‍💻

Human Review on Every Upload

No video goes live without a trained reviewer watching it in full. Every. Single. One.

🔍

Multi-Layer Moderation

AI pre-screening + Human review + Editorial sign-off. Three gates before your child sees it.

🚨

Instant Removal Protocol

Any flagged content is removed within minutes. Parents can flag, and we act immediately.

🤝

Creator Accountability

Every creator on our platform signs a strict content covenant. Violations mean permanent removal.
