Our Story

Built by people who lived it

Totspark was born at Comic Con. Four friends volunteering together, spotting something India was missing: a joyful, dedicated space for families and their toddlers.

What started as a shared dream turned into India's first entertainment platform built entirely for kids and families, where creators, artists, and performers bring joy to young audiences across every city, every home, every little screen.

  • 🎨
    Imagination first: We believe creativity is the most precious gift you can give a child.
  • 🧡
    Memories over milestones: We know how precious every laugh, giggle, and first word truly is.
  • ⭐
    Closer to you: Bringing creators, artists, and performers to your cities and homes.

How It Works

Built differently, on purpose

Every design decision was made with one question in mind: is this good for the child?

🤲

Handpicked Content

Every single video on our platform is individually reviewed by our team. No algorithm decides what your child sees. Real humans do, every time.

🔒

Age-Gated by Design

Content is tagged and locked by age group. A 4-year-old simply cannot access content meant for a 10-year-old. It's enforced at the system level.

⚡

Short-Form Magic

Designed for short attention spans in the best way, engaging, educational micro-content that leaves them inspired, not endlessly scrolling.

🌍

India-First Creators

We champion Indian creators, educators, artists, and performers. Content that reflects Indian culture, languages, and the diverse world your child lives in.

📊

Parent Dashboard

Full visibility for parents. See what your child watched, how long, and set daily time limits for complete control without hovering over their shoulder.

🎓

Learning Built In

Entertainment that teaches. Every piece of content goes through an educational value check. Fun is mandatory, but so is growth.

Safety First, Always

Zero compromise.
Zero tolerance.

Other platforms moderate at scale with algorithms. We moderate with people, process, and a parent's instinct. There is no grey area.

🧑‍💻

AI Review on Every Upload

Every video is scanned by AI before going live. 100% reviewed. Every. Single. One.

🔍

Multi-Layer Moderation

AI pre-screening + Human review + Editorial sign-off. Three gates before your child sees it.

🚨

Instant Removal Protocol

Any flagged content is removed within minutes. Parents can flag, and we act immediately.

🤝

Creator Accountability

Every creator signs a strict content covenant. Violations mean permanent removal.
