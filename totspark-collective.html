About Us

A New Standard for Children's Experiences

Totspark Collective was born from a simple belief — that every child deserves access to world-class experiences that nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence. We are India's first exclusive kids tech and event management collective, built by parents, educators, and innovators who refused to settle for ordinary.

We don't just plan events. We architect memories, build skills, and connect families to a curated ecosystem of premium experiences designed specifically for the young and the extraordinary.
